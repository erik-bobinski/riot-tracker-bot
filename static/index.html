riot-tracker

A Discord bot that posts League of Legends and VALORANT match results automatically.

Players link their Riot account to the bot once. After each linked player finishes a game, the bot polls the Riot and VALORANT match APIs and posts a summary of the match — mode, map, duration, score, and every player's champion/agent and K/D — directly into the server's Discord channel. No dashboard or website login is involved; all functionality happens inside Discord.
